Leader in high-current systems

This is us.

BRAR Elettromeccanica is among the world's leading manufacturers of integrated high-current solutions — flexible electrical connections, busbars, electrode arms and water-cooled systems for steelmaking, electroplating, automotive and heavy industry.

In India, BRAR supports major steel producers, automotive plants and industrial facilities with engineering, manufacturing and lifecycle services — backed by 50 years of global expertise since 1974.
